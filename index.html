Not at all! My workshops are designed to be accessible for everyone, regardless of background. I provide step-by-step guidance, interactive prompts, and flexible activities that allow both beginners and experienced participants to engage fully.

I begin by researching your organization and participants to understand context, needs, and accessibility. From there, I consult closely with your liaison to align with deeper cultural context, logistical constraints, and what will resonate most with your audience.

Participants walk away with new skills in creative expression, confidence in using technology for storytelling, and a deeper sense of community. This could look like collaborative poems, interactive digital art, or simply stronger connections between people who rarely create together.

All workshops are free of charge during my first developmental year. Timing is flexible, ranging from 30 minutes to 3 hours depending on your audience’s needs and the space available. I provide the materials and technologies, tailoring them to the poetic and technical background of participants.
